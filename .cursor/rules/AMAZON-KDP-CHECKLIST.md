- [ ] **Royalty:** 35%
- [ ] **No delivery cost** deduction

**Recommended eBook Pricing:**

**If keeping at ~33K words (novella):**
- **$2.99** - Competitive, 70% royalty ($2.04/sale)
- **$3.99** - Fair for quality novella ($2.73/sale)

**If expanding to 50K+ words:**
- **$3.99** - Series starter pricing ($2.73/sale)
- **$4.99** - Full novel pricing ($3.43/sale)

### Paperback Pricing

**Cost Calculation:**
- Amazon printing cost = ~$4.00 (for 130-page 5x8" book)
- Your royalty = List price - Printing cost - 40% of list price

**Examples:**
- $12.99 list ‚Üí $4.00 printing ‚Üí 40% cut ($5.20) = **$3.79 royalty**
- $14.99 list ‚Üí $4.00 printing ‚Üí 40% cut ($6.00) = **$4.99 royalty**

**Recommended Paperback Price:**
- **$12.99** - Competitive for novella length
- **$14.99** - Standard for novel

### Launch Strategy

**Option 1: Launch at $0.99 (35% royalty)**
- Build reviews quickly
- Climb bestseller lists
- Raise to $3.99-4.99 after 30 days

**Option 2: Launch at $3.99-4.99 (70% royalty)**

**Recommendation:** Launch at **$3.99** (or $2.99 if staying at 33K words)

## üéØ KDP SELECT vs. WIDE DISTRIBUTION

### KDP Select (Exclusive to Amazon)

**Requirements:**
- [ ] 90-day exclusivity (can't sell on other platforms)
- [ ] Auto-renews every 90 days (can opt out before renewal)

**Benefits:**
- [ ] **KU/KOLL:** Kindle Unlimited readers (earn per page read)
- [ ] **Countdown Deals:** Limited-time price promotions
- [ ] **Free Book Promotions:** Up to 5 days free per 90-day period
- [ ] **70% royalty in more countries**

**Earnings Potential:**
- Base royalty: 70% of $3.99 = $2.73/sale
- KU pages read: $0.004/page √ó ~140 pages = $0.56 per full read
- Free promo downloads: Great for reviews/visibility

### Wide Distribution (All Platforms)

**Platforms:**
- Amazon KDP
- Draft2Digital (aggregator for Apple, Kobo, Nook, etc.)
- IngramSpark (libraries, bookstores)
- Direct to Apple Books, Google Play, Kobo

**Benefits:**
- [ ] Reach readers on all platforms
- [ ] Not dependent on single retailer
- [ ] Keep all rights
- [ ] More control

**Recommendation for Book One:**
Start with **KDP Select** for 90 days:
- Build reviews and visibility
