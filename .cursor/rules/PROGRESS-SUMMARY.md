## üìã REMAINING TASKS FOR AMAZON KDP LAUNCH

### üî¥ **CRITICAL (Must Do Before Publishing)**

#### 1. **Professional Editing** - HIGHEST PRIORITY
- **Type:** Copyediting minimum (grammar, punctuation, consistency)
- **Cost:** $800-$1,500 for 33K words
- **Timeline:** 2-3 weeks
- **Optional but recommended:** Developmental edit ($1,500-$3,000)
- **Where to find:** Reedsy, Upwork, referrals from writing groups

**Why critical:** Amazon readers expect professional quality. Typos and errors = bad reviews.

#### 2. **Cover Design**
- **Step 1:** Generate concept using COVER-ART-PROMPT.md
  - Use Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion
  - Generate 10-15 variations
  - Cost: $20-50 for AI generation
  
- **Step 2:** Refine with professional designer
  - Add typography (title, author name, subtitle)
  - Adjust composition and colors
  - Create eBook version (1600x2400px) AND print version (full wrap)
  - Cost: $300-$800 for professional refinement
  - Alternative: DIY with Canva ($13/month) if budget tight

**Why critical:** Cover is the #1 factor in whether someone clicks on your book.

#### 3. **Manuscript Formatting**
- **eBook formatting:**
  - Convert to .epub or upload .doc/.docx
  - Proper chapter headings
  - Linked Table of Contents
  - No page numbers or headers/footers
  - Tools: Vellum ($249), Atticus ($147), Draft2Digital (free), Calibre (free)
  
- **Print formatting:**
  - PDF with proper margins for 5"x8" trim size
  - Estimated 130-140 pages
  - Include copyright page, dedication (optional)
  - Use KDP print templates

**Why critical:** Poor formatting = bad reading experience = bad reviews.

#### 4. **Author Bio**
- **Length:** 100-250 words
- **Include:** 
  - Brief background
  - Why you wrote this book
  - What you do when not writing
  - Where you live
  - Series information
  - Contact/website (if you have one)
  
**Template:**
```
[Your Name] is a [background]. THE FREQUENCY is their debut novel, 
born from [inspiration - meditation practice? consciousness exploration? 
vision of unified humanity?]. When not writing consciousness sci-fi, 
they [hobbies/interests]. They live in [location] and are currently 
working on Book Two of The Frequency Trilogy.

Connect: [website] | [social media]
```

**Why critical:** Readers want to know who you are. Builds connection and trust.
