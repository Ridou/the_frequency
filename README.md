## Story Synopsis

Christian Rand discovers he can tap into "the frequency"—a universal consciousness that connects all things. After amplifying baseball players during Game 7 of the World Series, he's recruited by The Apostles, a group of twelve who believe he might be prophesied to unite humanity. But the Controllers, a powerful faction who believe in guiding humanity from the shadows, want him for their own purposes.
