# THE FREQUENCY - Elevator Pitches

## 30-Second Pitch

A tech support worker discovers he can tap into universal consciousness when he accidentally helps the Dodgers win the World Series. Recruited by twelve immortal guides, he learns he's fulfilling an ancient prophecy—unite Earth's consciousness and prepare humanity for galactic citizenship. When Earth fully awakens, the planet gifts humanity an indestructible metal that can only be shaped by unified minds, launching us into the space age and toward first contact with alien intelligence.
